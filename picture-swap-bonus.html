Picture Swap - Traversing!

Press the buttons below to make the pictures swap.

Create 3 divs that each look like a picture Frame;
each one should have a unique background image and a button
underneath that swaps the image from the frame. Use Traversing
functions of jQuery to accomplish this.

The Rules are as follows:
The left frame swaps to the right and takes the image from the frame in the center.
The center frame swaps randomly to either the left or right.
The right frame swaps to the left and takes the image from the frame in the center.
